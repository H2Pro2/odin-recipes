Lasagna

Description

The pinnacle of italian cuisine. Lasagna.

No other dish is as recognisable as the lasagna. The ragu alla bolognese , the sheets of pasta,

combined with a white sauce and to top it all of grana pardano make for one of the greatest mixes of italian food.

Ingredients

Ragu alla Bolognese

  • 1kg of mixed minced pork and cow meat
  • 1,5kg of tomatoe sauce
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 large carrots
  • Red Wine to taste
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 4 Bay leafs
  • Olive Oil
Preparation

You heat up a pan with some drops of your olive oil. You add some finely chopped onions.

And wait for them to get translucent. Right now you want to add in your meat, stir a bit and wait till the meat is gray.

Add one shot of red wine and wait till some of the alcohol has evaporated. At some point you can add your tomatoe sauce to the pan.

After an initial cook you can pretty much add your carrots, garlic and Bay leafs. Salt and pepper to taste and that's it.

Now you just let it simmer for atleast 2 hours while stiring it sometimes.

White Sauce

  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter
Preparation

Heat up some butter in a pan untill it is completly molten.

Add a bit of flour and stir like a madman. Now slowly start to add milk until it has a oily texture.

Assembling the lasagna

Spread some butter in the form you want to use for your lasagna and then repeat the following steps.
  1. Pour in some Ragu and spread it.
  2. Pour in just enough white sauce so it covers the ragu.
  3. Place your Pasta on top.
  4. Repeat

You should finish your lasagna with a layer of ragu and white sauce. Now grate some Grana Pardano over it till everything is covered.

Now bake it in an oven for atleast 30 minutes at 180Â°C.

That is it. You've now made a wonderful lasagna.
